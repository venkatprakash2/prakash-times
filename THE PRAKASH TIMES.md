# **THE PRAKASH TIMES**

## **Personal Digital Newspaper & Magazine for Dad**

---

# **Project Vision**

This project is not a birthday website, portfolio website, resume website, or family photo album.

It is a personalized digital newspaper and magazine built specifically for Dad, combining his interests, memories, career, family, and knowledge into a single immersive experience.

The birthday serves only as the entry point through a special edition reveal. After that, the website should feel like a personal newspaper that can continue to be used and explored long after the birthday.

The website should answer the question:

"What would a premium digital newspaper look like if it were created specifically for Dad?"

---

# **Core Philosophy**

The website should feel like a blend of:

* Financial Times (10%)  
* The Economist (10%)  
* The Hindu (15%)  
* Apple storytelling pages (25%)  
* Spotify Wrapped (30%)  
* Modern AI dashboard (10%)

The website should feel:

* Personal  
* Premium  
* Intelligent  
* Warm  
* Visual  
* Interactive

The website should NOT feel:

* Corporate  
* Generic  
* Like a resume  
* Like a productivity dashboard  
* Like a family archive  
* Like a traditional newspaper replica

---

# **Content Philosophy**

The website is FOR Dad, not ABOUT Dad.

Only a small portion of the website should focus directly on him.

Approximate focus:

* 25% Dad-specific content  
* 75% Content tailored to his interests

The website should function like a personalized newspaper where every section is inspired by something he enjoys.

---

# **Design Language**

## **Color Palette**

Primary:

* Black  
* White

Secondary:

* Warm coffee tones  
* Espresso brown  
* Light coffee paper background

Accent colors should be minimal and rare.

---

## **Visual Style**

Modern newspaper-inspired design.

Characteristics:

* Strong typography  
* Editorial layouts  
* Large photography  
* Grid-based composition  
* White space  
* Magazine-style storytelling

Target ratio:

* 70% visual  
* 30% text

Every major text element should be accompanied by visuals.

---

# **Coffee Integration Philosophy**

Coffee should NOT be a dedicated page.

Coffee should be integrated throughout the entire website.

Examples:

* Coffee paper textures  
* Espresso-colored accents  
* Coffee ring transition effects  
* Steam-like hover animations  
* Brewing-inspired loading animations  
* Warm paper backgrounds

Coffee is part of the website's identity, not its content.

---

# **AI Integration Philosophy**

AI should be mostly invisible.

The website should not feel like an AI website.

AI should enhance features behind the scenes.

Examples:

* News article summaries  
* Visual infographic generation  
* Intelligent content recommendations  
* Curated AI tools directory

AI should feel like infrastructure, not the main attraction.

---

# **Animation Philosophy**

Animation level:

Heavy but elegant.

Use:

* Framer Motion  
* Smooth page transitions  
* Parallax effects  
* Scroll storytelling  
* Layered movement

Avoid:

* Flashy animations  
* Excessive motion  
* Distracting effects

Animations should feel premium and editorial.

---

# **Experience Goals**

When Dad finishes exploring the website, he should feel:

* Appreciated  
* Understood  
* Curious  
* Inspired  
* Proud  
* Connected to family

The ultimate goal is to create something he continues visiting after his birthday because it remains genuinely useful, interesting, and enjoyable.
